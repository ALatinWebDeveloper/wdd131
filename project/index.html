Welcome to
Monster Hunter Rise!

Embark on an epic journey in the world of Monster Hunter Rise, where you become a skilled hunter tasked with defending the village of Kamura from ferocious monsters. Explore lush environments, from dense forests to arid deserts, each teeming with unique creatures and challenges.

Key features

Dynamic Combat

Utilize a variety of weapons and master the new Wirebug mechanic to traverse the terrain and unleash powerful attacks.

Companions

Hunt alongside your trusty Palamute and Palico companions, each offering unique support in battles.

Customization

Craft and upgrade your gear using materials gathered from defeated monsters to enhance your abilities and appearance.

Multiplayer

Team up with friends in online multiplayer mode to tackle the most formidable foes together.
